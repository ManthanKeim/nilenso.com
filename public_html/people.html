nilenso

Nilenso is an employee-owned software cooperative with offices in Bangalore and Vancouver. We're excited about the software industry of 2013. Never has the world been so connected. Never has so much data been available. Never have so many open technologies enabled us to build new businesses in a matter of weeks.

We build web services and web applications -- primarily in Ruby and Rails. But we also love Javascript, Node.js, and Clojure. We also jump at any chance to get our hands dirty with native development on iOS and Android. If you've heard of us and think you'd like to work with us, take a look at our team and then give us a call! We'd love to hear from you. You seem cool.
